## ðŸ“– Getting Your Stripe Keys (If You Have a Stripe Account)

### Step 1: Log in to Stripe
1. Go to https://dashboard.stripe.com
2. Log in with your Stripe account credentials

### Step 2: Find Your API Keys
1. Click on **"Developers"** in the left sidebar
2. Click on **"API keys"**
3. You'll see two types of keys:

### Step 3: Copy Both Keys

**Key 1: Publishable Key**
- Look for the key that starts with `pk_test_...` (for testing) or `pk_live_...` (for live payments)
- Click the **"Reveal test key"** or **"Reveal live key"** button (looks like an eye icon)
- Click the **copy icon** to copy it
- Label this as: "Publishable Key"

**Key 2: Secret Key**
- Look for the key that starts with `sk_test_...` (for testing) or `sk_live_...` (for live payments)
- Click the **"Reveal test key"** or **"Reveal live key"** button
- Click the **copy icon** to copy it
- Label this as: "Secret Key"

### Step 4: Send Them to Us Securely
See the "How to Send Your Keys Securely" section below.

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What are these keys used for?
A: These keys allow your Apinlero store to process payments securely. When a customer buys from your store, the payment goes directly to YOUR Stripe account and YOUR bank account.

### Q: Will Apinlero have access to my money?
A: No! Payments go directly from your customers to YOUR Stripe account to YOUR bank account. We never touch your money. The keys just allow the payment process to work.

### Q: What's the difference between test and live keys?
A:
- **Test keys** (`pk_test_...` and `sk_test_...`): For testing payments without real money
- **Live keys** (`pk_live_...` and `sk_live_...`): For accepting real customer payments

We recommend starting with test keys first, then switching to live keys when you're ready.

### Q: Are these keys safe to share with you?
A: Yes, but only if shared securely (as described above). We store them encrypted and only use them server-side to process payments on your behalf. We never expose them publicly.

### Q: Can I change these keys later?
A: Yes! You can regenerate new keys in your Stripe dashboard anytime and send us the new ones.

### Q: How much does Stripe charge?
A: Stripe typically charges 1.4% + 20p per transaction in the UK. You can see exact fees in your Stripe dashboard. We (Apinlero) don't take any additional payment processing fees.
