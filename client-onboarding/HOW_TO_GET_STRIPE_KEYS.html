Q: Will Apinlero have access to my money?

A: No! Payments go directly from customers to YOUR Stripe account to YOUR bank. We never touch your money.

Q: What's the difference between test and live keys?

A: Test keys (pk_test_ and sk_test_) are for testing with fake money. Live keys (pk_live_ and sk_live_) are for real payments. We recommend starting with test keys!

Q: How much does Stripe charge?

A: Stripe charges 1.4% + 20p per transaction in the UK. Apinlero doesn't take any additional fees.
