## 2. Managing Products

### Viewing Products
1. Click the **Inventory** tab in the dashboard
2. You'll see all your products in a grid
3. Use the search box to find specific products

### Updating Stock
1. Find the product in the Inventory tab
2. Click the **+** or **-** buttons to adjust stock
3. Changes save automatically

### Adding New Products (Barcode Scanner)
1. Click **Scan Barcode** in the Inventory tab
2. Allow camera access if prompted
3. Hold the barcode in front of your camera
4. If the product exists, it will show details
5. If it's new, you can add it:
   - Enter product name
   - Set price
   - Choose category
   - Add initial stock quantity
   - Click **Save**

### Setting Bulk Pricing
For wholesale discounts:
1. Click on a product in Inventory
2. Click **Edit Bulk Pricing**
3. Set quantity tiers (e.g., 10+ units = 10% off)
4. Save changes

### Managing Product Images
Products without images show a placeholder. To add images:
1. Upload your product images to a hosting service (e.g., Imgur, Cloudinary)
2. Contact support to update image URLs

## 3. Processing Orders

### Viewing New Orders
1. Check the **Overview** tab for "Pending Orders" count
2. Or click the **Orders** section in Overview
3. New orders show status: **Pending**

### Order Details
Click on any order to see:
- Customer name and phone number
- Delivery address
- Ordered items with quantities
- Total amount
- Payment method
- Any special notes

### Updating Order Status
1. Find the order in the Orders table
2. Click the **Status** dropdown
3. Change from:
   - **Pending** → **Confirmed** (when you've acknowledged the order)
   - **Confirmed** → **Delivered** (when delivered/collected)

### Generating Delivery Links
For drivers to confirm delivery:
1. Click on a Confirmed order
2. Click **Generate Delivery Link**
3. Copy the link and send to your driver via WhatsApp
4. Driver opens link and confirms delivery

### Filtering Orders
Use the filter buttons to view:
- All Orders
- Pending (needs action)
- Confirmed (being prepared)
- Delivered (completed)

## 5. Daily Operations

### Morning Checklist
Each morning:
1. [ ] Check **Pending Orders** - confirm new orders
2. [ ] Review **Low Stock Alerts** - restock if needed
3. [ ] Check **AI Insights** for recommendations
4. [ ] Review any **Expiring Products**

### During the Day
- Process orders as they come in
- Update stock when receiving deliveries
- Mark orders as Delivered when completed

### End of Day
1. [ ] Ensure all delivered orders are marked **Delivered**
2. [ ] Check tomorrow's delivery schedule
3. [ ] Review today's sales in Overview
4. [ ] Note any products to reorder

### Weekly Tasks
- Review **Channel Performance** - which channels bring most orders
- Check **Customer Predictions** - identify at-risk customers
- Review **AI Insights** trends
- Plan promotions for upcoming events

## 6. Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't log in?**
- Check your email and password are correct
- Try clearing browser cache
- Contact support for password reset

**Products not showing on storefront?**
- Make sure product `is_active` is true
- Check stock is greater than 0
- Refresh the storefront page

**Orders not appearing?**
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the dashboard
- Orders sync in real-time - wait a few seconds

**Barcode scanner not working?**
- Allow camera permissions in browser
- Ensure good lighting
- Hold barcode steady in view

**WhatsApp messages not sending?**
- Check Twilio credits are topped up
- Verify customer phone number format (UK: starts with 07)
- Contact support if issues persist
