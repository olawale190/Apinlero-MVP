## 1. Innovation Evidence

### 1.1 Problem Being Solved
Small and medium businesses in the UK, particularly in the African & Caribbean retail sector, struggle with:
- Managing orders from multiple channels (WhatsApp, phone, walk-in, web)
- Lack of affordable technology solutions tailored to their needs
- Manual order tracking leading to errors and lost revenue
- No access to business intelligence or AI insights

### 1.2 Innovative Solution
Àpínlẹ̀rọ provides:
- **Multi-channel order management** - Unified dashboard for all sales channels
- **AI-powered insights** - Real-time business intelligence (top products, revenue trends, urgent actions)
- **White-label storefront** - Each business gets their own branded online store
- **Mobile-first design** - Optimized for how SME owners actually work

### 1.3 Technical Innovation
- Built with modern tech stack (React, TypeScript, Supabase, Vite)
- Real-time database synchronization
- AI summary generation for business insights
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop
- White-label architecture for multi-tenant deployment
