## 4. REFERRAL & INCENTIVE PROGRAMME

### 4.1 Programme Overview

The Àpínlẹ̀rọ Referral Programme incentivises existing customers to recommend our platform to other retailers, creating a sustainable, low-cost acquisition channel that leverages trust within close-knit business communities.

**Programme Philosophy:**
- Reward loyalty and advocacy
- Create genuine incentives without appearing desperate
- Build community around shared success
- Keep incentive costs below 6% of revenue

### 4.2 Incentive Structure

#### Referral Credits
| Action | Credit Value | Description |
|--------|--------------|-------------|
| Successful Referral | £50 | Credit applied when referred customer completes first month |
| Referred Customer Bonus | £25 | New customer receives £25 off first invoice |
| **Total per Referral** | **£75** | Combined cost per successful referral |

**Terms & Conditions:**
- Credits are non-transferable and non-cashable
- Credits applied to future invoices only
- Maximum 3 referral credits per customer per month
- Referred customer must be new to Àpínlẹ̀rọ
- Referring customer must be in good standing (paid up to date)

#### Social Proof Credits
| Action | Credit Value | Description |
|--------|--------------|-------------|
| Social Media Post | £10 | Share experience on Facebook/Instagram with tag |
| Video Testimonial | £25 | 30-60 second video testimonial |
| Written Case Study | £20 | Participate in detailed case study |
| Google Review | £10 | Leave honest review on Google Business |
| **Maximum per Quarter** | **£45** | Cap on social proof credits |

**Quality Requirements:**
- Posts must be genuine and authentic
- Minimum 50 words for written content
- Video must show actual platform use or business context
- All content subject to approval before credit issued

#### Loyalty Credits
| Milestone | Credit Value | Description |
|-----------|--------------|-------------|
| 6-Month Anniversary | £20 | Thankyou credit for loyalty |
| 12-Month Anniversary | £50 | Annual loyalty reward |
| 24-Month Anniversary | £100 | Premium loyalty reward |
| Annual Subscription | 1 month free | Equivalent to £139 discount |

### 4.3 Credit Mechanics

**How Credits Work:**
1. Credits accumulate in customer account
2. Credits auto-apply to next invoice
3. Credits never expire (while customer is active)
4. Credits cannot exceed invoice amount (no negative invoices)
5. Unused credits carry forward

**Example Scenario:**
- Retailer A refers Retailer B
- Retailer B signs up and pays first invoice
- Retailer A receives £50 credit
- Retailer B receives £25 credit on first invoice
- Next month, Retailer A's invoice is £139 - £50 = £89

## 5. SOCIAL PROOF STRATEGY

### 5.1 Importance of Social Proof

In close-knit ethnic business communities, purchasing decisions are heavily influenced by peer recommendations and visible success stories. Traditional advertising is met with scepticism, while word-of-mouth from trusted community members carries significant weight.

**Key Insight:** A single credible testimonial from a respected community retailer is worth more than £10,000 in advertising spend.

### 5.2 Social Proof Assets

#### Video Testimonials
**Target:** 8-10 video testimonials by end of Year 1

**Format:**
- Length: 60-90 seconds
- Content: Problem → Solution → Results
- Setting: In their shop/business
- Style: Authentic, not scripted

**Production Process:**
1. Identify satisfied customers with strong results
2. Brief on key talking points (not script)
3. Film on smartphone (authentic feel)
4. Light editing for quality
5. Subtitle for accessibility
6. Distribute across channels

**Distribution Channels:**
- Website testimonials page
- Instagram Reels / Facebook Videos
- WhatsApp status shares
- Community group shares
- Sales demo presentations

#### Written Case Studies
**Target:** 4 detailed case studies by end of Year 1

**Format:**
- Length: 800-1,200 words
- Structure: Challenge → Solution → Implementation → Results
- Include specific metrics and quotes
- Professional design with photos

**Case Study Template:**
1. Business Background (size, products, location)
2. Challenges Before Àpínlẹ̀rọ
3. Why They Chose Àpínlẹ̀rọ
4. Implementation Experience
5. Quantified Results (waste reduction, time saved, revenue impact)
6. Future Plans
7. Recommendation Quote

#### Customer Metrics Dashboard
**Publicly Shareable Stats:**
- Total food waste prevented (kg)
- Average time saved per week
- Customer satisfaction score
- Collective revenue protected

**Updated:** Monthly on website and marketing materials

### 5.3 Social Media Strategy

**Platforms (Priority Order):**
1. **WhatsApp Status** - Primary channel for community reach
2. **Instagram** - Visual storytelling and testimonials
3. **Facebook** - Community group engagement
