## Executive Summary

This Go-to-Market (GTM) Plan outlines how Àpínlẹ̀rọ will acquire its first 400 customers over 3 years using a **community-led growth strategy** that achieves exceptional unit economics (LTV:CAC 23:1, CAC £150).

**Core Strategy:** Leverage ethnic business communities and sector associations for low-cost, high-trust customer acquisition.

```
                    AWARENESS
                   (1000/month)
                        │
                        ▼
                    INTEREST
            Website visit, content engagement
                   (300/month)

                    (2/month)
```

### 3.2 Conversion Rates

| Stage | Conversion Rate | Target (Year 1) |

| Trial → Active User | 70% | Maintain |
| Active User → Paid | 40% | Improve to 50% |
| Paid → Referrer | 30% | Improve to 40% |
| **Overall Visitor → Paid** | **1.4%** | **Improve to 2.5%** |

### 3.3 Sales Motion by Segment

| Segment | Sales Motion | Touchpoints | Cycle Length |
|---------|--------------|-------------|--------------|
| **Solo (£150)** | Self-serve | Website, trial, email nurture | 14 days |
| **Starter (£250)** | Product-led | Trial + 1 sales call | 21 days |
| **Growth (£350)** | Sales-assisted | Demo + 2-3 calls + trial | 30-45 days |

### 3.4 Sales Playbook

#### Initial Contact (Inbound Lead)

**Response Time:** <2 hours during business hours

**First Touch Email:**
```
Subject: Thanks for checking out Àpínlẹ̀rọ, [Name]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for signing up for a free trial of Àpínlẹ̀rọ!

I noticed you're in the [sector] business. We've helped similar businesses
[specific benefit - e.g., "reduce WhatsApp order chaos by 90%"].

Would you have 15 minutes this week for a quick call? I'd love to understand
your specific challenges and show you exactly how Àpínlẹ̀rọ can help.

[Calendar link]

Best,
[Name]
```

#### Discovery Call Framework (15 minutes)

1. **Rapport (2 min):** Connect personally, acknowledge their business
2. **Situation (3 min):** Current tools, team size, order volume
3. **Problem (5 min):** Biggest operational challenges, impact on business
4. **Implication (3 min):** Cost of status quo (time, money, stress)
5. **Next Step (2 min):** Schedule demo or confirm trial focus areas

#### Demo Framework (30 minutes)

1. **Recap Discovery (3 min):** Confirm challenges heard
2. **Solution Overview (5 min):** High-level how Àpínlẹ̀rọ solves
3. **Tailored Demo (15 min):** Focus on their specific use case
4. **ROI Discussion (5 min):** Concrete savings/gains they'll see
5. **Close (2 min):** Trial activation or subscription

#### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "What's the cost of one missed order or scheduling error? Most customers save 10+ hours/week - at minimum wage that's £100/week. Pays for itself." |
| "Need to think about it" | "Completely understand. What specific concerns would you want to think through? Often we can address those now." |
| "We use spreadsheets fine" | "Many of our best customers said the same. Can I show you the difference [case study business] saw in their first month?" |
| "My team won't use it" | "We designed it for non-technical teams. [Persona name] said the same - his engineers adopted it within a week because it's simpler than paper." |
| "Need to check with partner/boss" | "Of course. Would it help if I joined a call with them? Or I can prepare a summary document showing the ROI for your situation." |

## 4. Customer Success & Retention

### 4.1 Onboarding Process

| Day | Action | Owner | Success Metric |
|-----|--------|-------|----------------|
| 0 | Welcome email + quick start guide | Automated | Opened |
| 1 | Onboarding call scheduled | CS | Booked within 48h |
| 3 | Onboarding call (30 min setup) | CS | Core features configured |
| 7 | Check-in email | Automated | Engagement check |
| 14 | Success call (trial end) | CS | Conversion decision |
| 30 | First month review | CS | Health score >70 |

### 4.2 Customer Health Score

| Factor | Weight | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Login frequency | 25% | Daily = 100, Weekly = 50, Monthly = 20 |
| Feature usage | 25% | % of subscribed features used |
| Support tickets | 20% | 0-1 = 100, 2-3 = 50, 4+ = 20 |
| NPS score | 15% | Promoter = 100, Passive = 50, Detractor = 0 |
| Payment status | 15% | Current = 100, Late = 50, Failed = 0 |

**Health Score Actions:**

| Score | Status | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 80-100 | Healthy | Upsell opportunity, referral ask |
| 60-79 | Attention | Proactive check-in |
| 40-59 | At Risk | Urgent outreach, recovery plan |
| 0-39 | Critical | Executive intervention |

### 4.3 Retention Tactics

| Tactic | Trigger | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Re-engagement** | No login 7 days | Email: "We miss you" + tip |
| **Feature Education** | Low feature usage | Email: Tutorial for unused feature |
| **Success Celebration** | Milestone (100 orders) | Email: Congratulations + share |
| **Churn Prevention** | Health score <50 | Call: Understand issues, offer help |
| **Win-Back** | Cancelled <30 days | Email: Special offer to return |

### 4.4 Churn Analysis Framework

| Churn Reason | % of Churns | Prevention Strategy |
|--------------|-------------|---------------------|
| Not enough value | 30% | Better onboarding, feature education |
| Too complex | 25% | Simplify UX, more training |
| Price | 20% | Demonstrate ROI, offer annual discount |
| Business closed | 15% | N/A |
| Switched competitor | 10% | Feature parity, relationship building |

## 9. Risk Mitigation

### 9.1 GTM Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Community approach doesn't scale | Medium | High | Diversify channels, test paid earlier |
| Longer sales cycles than expected | Medium | Medium | Improve qualification, add nurture |
| Low trial conversion | Medium | High | Enhance onboarding, add sales touch |
| Competitor response | Low | Medium | Differentiate on community, Voice AI |
| Economic downturn | Medium | High | Emphasise cost savings, efficiency |

### 9.2 Contingency Plans

**If community acquisition underperforms:**
- Increase paid advertising budget by 50%
- Launch affiliate programme
- Add outbound sales motion

**If trial conversion is below 30%:**
- Implement required onboarding call
- Reduce trial length to 7 days with urgency
- Add in-trial success manager touch

**If churn exceeds 5% monthly:**
- Conduct churn interviews
- Implement win-back campaign
- Review pricing and packaging
