## 8. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 8.1 Right of Access (Article 15)
Request a copy of your personal data. We will respond within 30 days.

### 8.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 8.3 Right to Erasure (Article 17)
Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten") where:
- Data is no longer necessary
- You withdraw consent
- You object to processing
- Data was unlawfully processed

**Exceptions:** We may retain data for legal compliance or legitimate interests.

### 8.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)
Request limitation of processing while we verify accuracy or assess objections.

### 8.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON/CSV).

### 8.6 Right to Object (Article 21)
Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.

### 8.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)
- Request human review of automated decisions
- Express your point of view
- Contest decisions

### 8.8 Right to Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.

**To Exercise Your Rights:**
- Email: privacy@apinlero.com
- In-app: Settings > Privacy > Data Rights
- Response time: 30 days (extendable by 60 days for complex requests)
