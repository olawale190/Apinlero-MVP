### 4.1 Specialty Grocery

#### Key Setup Tasks

| Task | Priority | Typical Time |
|------|----------|--------------|
| Import product catalogue | High | 15-30 min |
| Set up WhatsApp ordering | High | 10 min |

| Set up delivery zones/fees | Medium | 5 min |

#### Success Milestones

| Day | Milestone |
|-----|-----------|
| 1 | First order received via WhatsApp |

#### Common Challenges

| Challenge | Solution |
|-----------|----------|

## 6. Escalation Process

### 6.1 When to Escalate

| Situation | Escalate To | Timeframe |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Technical issue blocking setup | Engineering | Immediate |
| Customer frustrated/angry | CS Manager | Within 1 hour |
| Enterprise/high-value customer | Account Executive | Same day |
| Feature request (major) | Product Team | Within 24 hours |
| Churn risk identified | CS Manager | Same day |
