## Important Notes

### Legal Documents (01, 02, 07)

**These templates require professional legal review before publication:**

- Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and DPA are templates
- Must be reviewed by a qualified UK solicitor
- Company details (address, company number, VAT) must be added
- May need customisation for specific business circumstances
- Laws change - should be reviewed annually

**Recommended:** Engage a solicitor specialising in tech/SaaS businesses. Budget: £500-£1,500 for review and customisation.

### Operational Documents (03-06)

These are ready to use internally:
- User Personas - guide product and marketing
- Go-to-Market Plan - execute acquisition strategy
- Customer Onboarding - train CS team
- Support & SLA - set up support operations
