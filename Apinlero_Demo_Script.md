### 3.2 The Innovation - Contextual Intelligence
**Open: Neo4j Aura (if time permits)**

"What makes Àpínlẹ̀rọ different is our three-layer innovation:

**Layer 1: Knowledge Graph**
Our Neo4j knowledge graph understands that 'red oil' = 'palm oil' = 'zomi' = 'epo pupa'. It knows Nigerian, Ghanaian, and Caribbean product names.

**Layer 2: Intelligent Parsing**
When a customer sends 'I want 2x red oil 5L and 3bag rice', our system automatically:
- Extracts: Palm Oil 5L (qty: 2), Rice Bag (qty: 3)
- Validates against inventory
- Calculates delivery zone from postcode
- Creates a structured order

This reduces order entry errors by **85%**.

**Layer 3: Predictive Operations**
The system learns patterns - like increased plantain orders before Nigerian Independence Day - and suggests stock reorders before items run out."
